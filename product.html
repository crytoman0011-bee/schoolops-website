The recovery operating system after fees become overdue.

SchoolOps gives school leaders a structured system for turning fee records into recovery actions, promises, follow-ups, escalations, and management visibility.

Focused on recovery. Not attendance, exams, LMS, or timetable.

Recovery Dashboard

See total receivable, overdue amount, collection rate, escalated cases, broken promises, and daily movement.

Promise Tracker

Record promised date, promised amount, fulfilled status, and broken commitments.
