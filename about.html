About

Built for the financial reality of private schools.

SchoolOps was created around one practical truth: schools do not just need more data. They need stronger recovery discipline.

Why SchoolOps exists

Private schools already have fee records in ERPs, Excel sheets, registers, and monthly reports. The real problem begins after fees become pending.

Parents delay. Staff follows up manually. Promises are forgotten. Principals receive updates late. Cash flow becomes unpredictable.

What we believe

SchoolOps exists to solve this exact operating gap. We are building the recovery layer above existing school systems — focused on overdue fees, parent promises, follow-up discipline, escalation, and principal-level visibility.

SchoolOps does not manage the school. SchoolOps protects the school’s monthly cash flow.
