Workflow

From overdue fee to recovered cash.

SchoolOps converts late fees into a disciplined operating workflow your team can actually follow.

Overdue

02

Reminder

03

Parent Response

Response is logged: paid, will pay, needs time, disputed, or no response.

04

The promised date and amount are recorded.

05

Follow-up

SchoolOps creates the next action and due follow-up task.

06

Broken Promise

If payment does not arrive, the case is flagged.

07

Escalation

Repeated delays move to principal-level review.

08

Recovered

Payment is updated and the recovery case closes.
